About

I'm a Sydney-based freelance web developer & multimedia designer, who gets excited about open source tech.

As a recent web design and development graduate I embrace technical challenges head-on. I enjoy working to create the best solution possible within the time constraints. I love working as part of a team to produce to spec high quality results.

I want to be involved in projects that I can be proud of, join an amazing team, and advance my design and development career. Take some time to look at my work, credentials, and if you're interested in helping me make this happen, please get in touch.

Skills

I have a wide variety of skills that can help bring your project to life. These include

I am competent in a wide variety of Adobe creative suite programs that assist in the production of beautiful and functional interface design. These include Photoshop, for which i am certified by Adobe as well as illustrator and InDesign. To assist in the production of your multimedia projects I am able to generate 3D models and animations in Cinema4D and import them into Premier Pro, Final Cut Pro or After Effects to create beautiful moving images.

To bring your project to life I use HTML5 and CSS3 and to create beautiful bespoke websites I hand code but i am Adobe certified in Dreamweaver if your project requires this. For animations, transitions and client side form validation i use JavaScript and JQuery.

In addition to my development skills I have also frequently worked to create easily manageable CMS websites in WordPress for small businesses. I am passionate about creating accessible and semantic mark-up. I also have experience using versioning the system Github.
